# Glossary

## Supplier
An entity or vendor that provides goods or services to the company.

## Cluster
A group of suppliers with similar behavior or characteristics, identified using clustering algorithms (e.g., K-Means).

## Risk Score
A numerical value representing the likelihood of supplier failure or disruption, based on multiple factors.

## EWMA (Exponentially Weighted Moving Average)
A statistical method that gives more importance to recent data points to detect trends or anomalies over time.

## Delay Rate
The frequency or percentage of late deliveries from a supplier.

## Defect Rate
The proportion of defective goods supplied.

## Reliability Score
A metric indicating how consistently a supplier meets expectations (delivery, quality, etc.).

## Synthetic Data
Artificially generated data used to simulate real-world supplier behavior.

## dbt (Data Build Tool)
A transformation tool used to clean, model, and structure data in the pipeline.

## Clustering
An unsupervised machine learning technique used to group similar data points.

## High-Risk Supplier
A supplier identified as likely to cause disruptions based on risk scoring.

## Workflow Automation (n8n)
Tool used to automate and orchestrate the data pipeline and notifications.

## Slack Notification
Automated alert sent to stakeholders when certain risk conditions are met.

## Dashboard
A visual interface (Evidence.dev) used to monitor supplier risk and insights.
